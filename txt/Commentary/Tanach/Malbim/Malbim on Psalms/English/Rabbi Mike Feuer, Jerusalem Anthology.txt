Verse 3

His tabernacle was in Shalem – In Jerusalem because there the masses settled, there was Gd’s tabernacle (sukkah) because a tabernacle is a temporary dwelling. 
And His dwelling place in Tzion. In Tzion the king, the Sanhedrin and the priest of Gd sat, there His dwelling was permanent because there was the Sanctuary of Gd.
