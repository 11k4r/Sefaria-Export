Ramban on Genesis

Chapter 1



Verse 1





… and it is already known that the four elements are one continuous solidity. Its unmoving base is the sphere of the land, the waters surrounds the land, the air surrounds the waters and the fire surrounds the air. The Torah describes the land taking form with the fire surrounding the mingled water and dust from above and the wind blowing and entering into the darkness and hovering over the waters. It appears to me that this point (which Gd created out of absolute nothing) in its taking on form and becoming bohu (in it there is) is what the Sages call “the foundation stone” (Yoma 54b) from which the world was founded…
