Halacha 4

<b>If the first two letters of the Name, etc.</b> this is void; the sages spoke of only (Shebuot, 35) if there was intention to write the full Name of God of the universe, or the Tetragrammaton, and he wrote only the first two letters thereof; but the first two letters without such intention are as the first two letters of Almighty or of Hosts which may be erased.<sup>*</sup><i class="footnote">Maimonides forbids it because it is part of the Tetragrammaton. (C. G.).</i>
