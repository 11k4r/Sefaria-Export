“And it was that on the day that Moses finished erecting the Mishkan…” (Bamidbar 7:1) This is what is written “King Shlomo made himself a palanquin…” (Shir haShirim 3:9) this is the world which is made like a type of canopy. ‘King Shlomo’ is the Holy One who made peace between fire and water, blending them together and making the firmament from them as it says “and Gd called the firmament heavens…” because it is fire and water. “…of the trees of Lebanon,” means that it (the world) was constructed from the place of the Bet HaMikdash. R’ Yosi bar Halifta said, why is it called Foundation Stone? Because from it the world was begun. This is what is written, “From Zion, the sum of beauty, God appeared.” (Tehillim 50:2)…
